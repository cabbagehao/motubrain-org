## Introduction

Motubrain.org is an independent bilingual information website available at [motubrain.org](https://motubrain.org). This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you visit the site.

## Information We Process

Motubrain.org does not provide account registration, paid features, uploads, or a database-backed user area.

We may process limited technical and analytics information:

1. **Basic usage analytics**
   - **What may be processed**: Page views, referrers, browser details, coarse location derived from IP address, and device information.
   - **Purpose**: To understand site traffic and improve the public information experience.

2. **Preference cookies or local storage**
   - **What may be stored**: Theme, locale, and similar interface preferences.
   - **Purpose**: To remember how you prefer to view the site.

3. **Server and security logs**
   - **What may be processed**: Request metadata such as IP address, user agent, timestamps, and error logs.
   - **Purpose**: To keep the site reliable, secure, and abuse-resistant.

## What We Do Not Collect

Motubrain.org does not intentionally collect account credentials, payment details, uploaded files, prompts, generated outputs, or user profile data because those features are not part of the site.

## Third-Party Services

The site may use hosting, analytics, DNS, and search-indexing services. These providers may process limited technical data under their own policies.

## Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data only with service providers that operate the site, or when required by law.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the site, analytics setup, or legal requirements change. The updated version will be posted on this page.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact [support@motubrain.org](mailto:support@motubrain.org).
