## Claim Guardrails

- Say Motubrain.org does not provide an API, demo, download, paid service, or ShengShu support.
- Say public materials reviewed by this site do not show a self-serve API, downloadable model, or public demo when that statement is tied to the reviewed source set.
- Do not say ShengShu will not provide access, that Motubrain is commercially available, or that benchmark claims are independently certified by Motubrain.org.
- Keep scores source-attributed to ShengShu, official pages, benchmark hosts, or screenshots already stored in the repo.
